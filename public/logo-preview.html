Design Rationale

The "T" Letterform

Bold white "T" for Teardown. Clean, professional, and immediately recognizable at any size.

Amber Scan Beam

Horizontal scanning line represents AI analysis revealing hidden damage - the core value proposition for shops.

Deep Navy Background

Conveys trust, authority, and professionalism - critical for a tool that interfaces with insurance companies.

Pulse Rings

Concentric rings on the scan endpoint suggest active, real-time AI processing - the tech-forward identity.
